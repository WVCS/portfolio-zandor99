The famous chef Gordon Ramsay (played by Gordon Ramsay), is driven mad by his contestants' terrible cooking and inability to find the lamb sauce, and he goes on a murderous rampage, killing all of his chefs and using their blood AS the lamb sauce. The makeshift lamb sauce ends up being unsatisfactory, and he hunts even more people down. The only person capable of taking down the ruler of kitchen hell is Julia Child, (played by Julia Child). Will the British cooking angel be able to defeat the cooking devil, or will his search for the lamb sauce claim even more lives.
